Apps

I have various projects in the works, but here are apps I have released:

Filter Art

Filter Art let's you quickly edit images to get cool results. You decide how you filter your images with Filter Art. Some images will naturally filter into cooler images so play around with it and you'll get a feel for what makes for a good filtered image. It is for both iOS and Mac.

It is coded in SwiftUI but dips into UIKit and AppKit where needed.


Clock Suite

Clock Suite let's you have the components of a clock app, each in their own window and with customizable colors across the whole app. It is for macOS and predates Apple's Clock app for mac.

It is coded in AppKit but could use an update to SwiftUI.


Spiffy Clock

Spiffy Clock is for iOS and let's you have a nice fullscreen clock with customizable colors and the choice to display seconds.

It is coded in React Native but could use an update to use TypeScript.
